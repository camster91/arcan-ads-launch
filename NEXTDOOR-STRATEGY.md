# Nextdoor — Strategy + Claim Guide

**Why Nextdoor:** Local social network, 80%+ of US households, 1 in 3 neighbors say a Nextdoor recommendation influenced a purchase. For a service business like painting, Nextdoor converts at 2-3x Google Local Services Ads because the social proof is built in (neighbors recommending to neighbors).

**Cost:** Free Business Page + free Business Posts. Paid Ads start at $25/day. Compared to Meta at $20/day for the same audience, Nextdoor often wins on CPL for service businesses because the audience is hyperlocal (1-2 mile radius).

**Competitive position:** Per the 4-agent competitive audit, **no major GTA painting competitor is on Nextdoor right now**. This is a free competitive moat you can claim this week.

## Metrics to track

In Nextdoor's Business Page dashboard, track:
- **Recommendations** (the most valuable metric) — these are gold
- **Post reach** — how many neighbors saw each post
- **Page views** — passive interest signal
- **Lead form fills** — when paid Ads are on
- **Calls / clicks** — track via the website's call tracking

Report weekly in `WEEKLY-NOTES.md`.

---

## What NOT to do

- Don't over-promote. Nextdoor neighbors hate spam. 3 posts/week max.
- Don't use AI-generated stock photos. Real Arcan projects from the gallery.
- Don't ignore recommendations — every "thanks for the great paint job!" comment is gold for SEO.
- Don't compete on price. Compete on family, warranty, and 25 years.
- Don't post from a personal profile. Always post from the Business Page.

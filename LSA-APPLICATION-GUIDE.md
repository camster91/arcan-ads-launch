# Google Local Services Ads (LSA) — Apply Today

**Why this matters:** LSA is the most underused channel in home services. It shows at the top of Google search results with a "Google Guaranteed" badge. Conversion rate is 2-3x regular Google Search Ads because of the built-in trust signal. For a painter in the GTA, LSA leads are the highest-quality leads you can buy.

**Why apply today:** Google does a background check + license verification that takes 3-6 weeks. If you wait until you "need" LSA leads, you'll have a 2-month gap with no leads. Apply now, get approved by end of June, and have LSA live before peak fall season (Sept-Oct).

**Cost:** You pay per LEAD, not per click. Typical home services LSA CPL: $30-80. You set a weekly budget cap (start at $200/week = ~$30/day). Google only charges for actual leads, not clicks or impressions.

## Step-by-step application (45-60 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Google Ads account (10 min)
1. Go to https://ads.google.com
2. Sign in with `info@arcanpainting.ca` (the same account that owns your Google Business Profile)
3. Switch to Expert Mode (the basic mode hides LSA setup)
4. Create a new campaign → Choose "Local services campaigns"

### Step 2: Business verification (15 min)
1. Enter business name, address, phone
2. Match against your Google Business Profile — they must be consistent
3. Enter business categories
4. Set service area: 25km radius (GTA) OR specific postal codes
5. Set weekly budget: start at $200/week (~$30/day)

### Step 3: License + insurance verification (15 min)
1. Upload business license (PDF or photo)
2. Upload WSIB clearance certificate
3. Upload liability insurance certificate
4. Upload owner's government ID
5. Upload business address verification

### Step 4: Background check (5 min)
1. Google runs a background check on all listed owners
2. Cost: $50-150 one-time per market
3. Takes 3-5 business days
4. You'll be notified by email

### Step 5: Set lead form and budget (10 min)
1. Lead form fields:
   - Full name
   - Phone (required)
   - Email
   - Project type (dropdown: Interior / Exterior / Cabinets / Wallpaper)
   - Project description (optional)
2. **Dispute policy:** Choose "Disputable" (lets you dispute bad leads)
3. **Review request:** Enable (so customers can leave reviews)
4. Set weekly budget: $200/week to start

### Step 6: Launch (5 min of clicks)
1. Click "Publish"
2. Ads go live after Google review (usually 24-48 hours)
3. You'll start receiving leads via email + phone

### Step 7: Set up lead disposition (important)
1. Go to https://ads.google.com → Local Services → Leads
2. Set up disposition statuses: Booked, Not Booked, Duplicate
3. Mark leads within 24 hours of contact
4. This trains Google's algorithm to send you better leads

## Common LSA pitfalls to avoid

### Pitfall 1: Not marking lead dispositions
- LSA's algorithm improves dramatically when you mark every lead
- Mark within 24 hours: Booked, Not Booked (with reason), Duplicate
- Skipping this = stuck with junk leads

### Pitfall 2: Responding slow
- LSA leads expect a call within 5 minutes
- Set up Google Voice or a forwarding number
- Don't use voicemail as the first response

### Pitfall 3: Setting weekly budget too high too fast
- Start at $200/week. After 30 days, double to $400 if CPL is good.
- Going from $200 to $2,000 in week 2 is a recipe for spam-tagged leads

### Pitfall 4: Not enabling reviews
- LSA reviews are critical for the algorithm
- After each completed job, send the customer the LSA review link
- Target: 5+ new reviews per month
- Reviews show on your LSA listing AND on Google Business Profile

### Pitfall 5: Wrong service categories
- If you list "House painter" but a lead comes in for "cabinet refinisher," you might not get the lead
- List all your services, not just the primary one
- Better to over-categorize and decline mismatches than to under-categorize and miss leads

## How to get reviews flowing (the LSA flywheel)

LSA's algorithm rewards high review counts + fast responses. After every job:

1. Send the customer a thank-you email with the LSA review link (auto-generated)
2. Ask in person: "If you were happy with the work, a Google review really helps us — it takes 30 seconds, here's the link"
3. Make it easy: send a text with the link on the day of completion
4. Target: 5+ reviews per month from new jobs alone
5. After 50 reviews, you become effectively un-Googleable from competitors

Reviews compound: more reviews → higher LSA position → more leads → more reviews.

# B2B Outreach — Property Managers + RE Agents

**Why B2B:** one property manager contract = $30K-100K/year recurring. One RE agent partner = 5-15 jobs/year. B2C campaigns bring $4-8K jobs; B2B brings $15-80K jobs. Lower volume, higher value.

**The Meta "RE Pre-Listing" campaign (Campaign 2) is the top of this funnel.** This doc is the rest of the funnel — what happens after a RE agent submits the Meta lead form.

---

## The B2B customer journey (different from B2C)

| | B2C (homeowner) | B2B (RE agent) |
|---|---|---|
| Job size | $3K-10K | $5K-30K |
| Decision time | 1-4 weeks | 1-7 days |
| Decision maker | Spouse + you | Agent alone (or with broker) |
| Trust signal | Reviews + warranty | Speed + partner rate + reliability |
| Re-purchase | Never (or 5+ years) | Every listing (every 2-3 months) |
| LTV | One job | 5-15 jobs over 2-3 years |

## Segment 1: Real estate agents (partner program)

### Lead capture
- Meta Campaign 2 (RE Pre-Listing) generates leads
- Custom lead form fields: brokerage, listings/year, callback time
- Lead lands in Arcan admin with `serviceType = "re_listing"` or similar marker

### Lead qualification (within 1 hour of submission)
- Arcan (or Gerardo) calls within 60 minutes — RE agents move fast
- Confirm: brokerage, area served, # of listings/year
- Pitch: "Partner rate" = 10% off standard pricing, 48-hour guarantee, dedicated account manager
- Send follow-up: one-page partner agreement + sample proposal

### Partner agreement template
- Partner rate: 10% off standard pricing
- Turnaround: 48 hours from paint-ready
- Payment: NET-15 from invoice date
- Minimum volume: no minimum (but tiered bonus at 5+ jobs/year)
- Referrals: 5% referral fee for any client the agent sends outside their listings

### Ongoing relationship
- Send monthly update: "You sent us X listings, we painted Y, here's what worked"
- Quarterly check-in: bring coffee + ask "how's the partnership going?"
- Annual: send a gift (Arcan-branded item, $50-100)

### Target list (build this)
- Top 50 brokerages in GTA (RE/MAX, Royal LePage, Century 21, etc.)
- 5-10 individual agents per brokerage = 250-500 agents
- Use the `arcan-painting` cold-email skill to seed this list
- First outreach via email, follow up with phone

## Segment 2: Property managers (recurring contracts)

### Who they are
- Residential property management companies (5-50 buildings under management)
- Condo corporations (HOAs, condo boards)
- Commercial property managers (office, retail, industrial)

### Job size
- $30K-100K/year for residential PMs (multiple unit turnovers, common areas)
- $50K-200K/year for condo corporations (annual exterior cycle, common areas)
- $50K-500K/year for commercial PMs (multi-tenant, recurring)

### How to find them
- Use the `arcan-painting` cold-email skill (already built, already loaded)
- Run the search queries for `property management companies [area]`
- Push prospects into the CRM
- Outreach follows the same 4-email sequence as agents

### Pitch (different from agents)
- "Overflow capacity" for when their in-house painter is booked
- "Backup vendor" status for emergency jobs
- "WSIB-covered, fully insured" (this matters more for PMs than for homeowners)
- "Itemized invoices" (PMs need clean accounting)
- "Multi-property discount" (5% off if they use us at 3+ properties)

## Segment 4: Home stagers (NEW — added 2026-06-01)

### Why bother
- Home stagers prepare properties for sale alongside RE agents
- They often need fast, high-quality painting to make a property "show-ready"
- Steady stream of small-to-medium jobs ($2K-8K each)
- Refer back to the RE agents they work with (cross-pollination)

### Target list (GTA home stagers)
1. Stage Right Home Staging
2. Set the Stage
3. Design Reyna
4. Urban Casa Staging
5. Dream Big Staging
6. (Search Google for "home staging Toronto" + neighborhood names for more)

### Pitch
- "Trade partner rate" = 15% off, 72-hour turnaround (faster than RE agent SLA)
- Pre-approval workflow: stager texts Arcan, gets a quote within 2 hours
- Direct billing: stager's invoice goes to their client, Arcan gets paid by stager

### Outreach sequence (cold email, 3 emails over 10 days)
Same templates as RE agent segment, with:
- Subject: "Quick paint turnaround for your staged properties?"
- Body: replace "real estate agent" with "home stager"
- Offer: 15% off (vs 10% for agents, because stagers send more volume)

## Segment 5: Insurance restoration companies (NEW — added 2026-06-01)

### Why bother
- Insurance companies pay for repainting after water/fire/storm damage
- High-margin, fast-turnaround work
- Steady flow from a few carrier relationships
- Less price-sensitive than B2C (insurance is paying)

### Target list
- ServiceMaster Restore (national franchise, GTA locations)
- Restoration 1 (national)
- PuroClean (national)
- ServPro (national)
- Local independents: search "fire and water damage restoration Toronto"

### Pitch
- "Insurance-approved vendor" status
- 48-hour response time on emergency calls
- Xactimate estimating (industry standard) — Arcan needs to learn this
- Per-job net 30 payment terms
- WSIB + liability already in place (typical insurance requirement)

### Outreach
- Cold call the franchise regional manager
- Ask to be added to their preferred vendor list
- One contract can mean 5-15 jobs/year per franchise location

## Segment 6: Real estate photographers (NEW — added 2026-06-01)

### Why bother
- RE photographers see every listing in their market
- They notice which listings look fresh vs dated
- Word-of-mouth referrals: "If you need paint before listing, call Arcan"
- Free relationship to build — invite them for coffee, send them a sample job

### Target list
- GTA RE photographers: search "real estate photographer Toronto" + neighborhood names
- Most work solo or in 2-3 person studios
- ~50-100 active RE photographers in the GTA

### Pitch
- "If you see a listing that needs a paint refresh before photos, send them my way"
- 10% referral fee for any closed job (paid after invoice clears)
- No formal contract — relationship-based
- Send them a $20 Starbucks card at Christmas as a thank you

### Outreach
- DM on Instagram (most RE photographers are IG-active)
- Offer to do a free "color consultation" for one of their current listings in exchange for a testimonial
- Don't pitch hard — these relationships are built over coffee, not emails

## Segment 3: Condo boards (slow but high-value)
- Annual contracts for exterior painting cycle (5-7 year intervals)
- Avg contract: $80K-200K for a mid-rise condo (10-20 stories)
- 1-2 contracts per year = a 7-figure revenue stream

### How to find them
- CMHC registry (public, free)
- Condo management companies (above list)
- Direct outreach: every condo in GTA has a management contact
- Attend the annual ACMO (Association of Condo Managers of Ontario) conference

### Pitch
- WSIB-covered + 5M liability insurance (condos require this)
- Reference list of 3+ condo clients
- Detailed proposal with line-item pricing
- Willing to attend AGM to present in person

### Outreach (much slower cycle)
- Condo board decisions are slow (AGM voting, multiple stakeholders)
- Expect 6-12 month sales cycle
- Best to land a relationship with the property manager first (Segment 2) — they often recommend painters to their condo board clients

## B2B lead tracking (in Arcan admin)

Add a new field to the `leads` table (or use existing `notes` field):
- `lead_type`: `re_agent` | `property_manager` | `condo_board` | `commercial` | `homeowner`
- `partner_status`: `prospect` | `active` | `inactive` | `vip`
- `lifetime_value`: $ (track every job they send)

Then in the admin leads view, filter by `lead_type = re_agent` to see all RE agent leads in one place.

## What you DON'T do

- Don't cold-call. Email only. Phone calls are for after they've responded.
- Don't blast generic templates. Personalize the first sentence with their brokerage or recent listing.
- Don't promise 48 hours and miss it. One bad promise = 5 lost listings.
- Don't compete on price with franchised painters (WOW 1 Day). Compete on reliability + family.
- Don't send 5 emails if they don't reply. 4 max.

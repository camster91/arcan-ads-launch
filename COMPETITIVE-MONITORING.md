# Competitive Monitoring — Weekly Ad Library Sweep

**Purpose:** know what your competitors are doing before they know you're doing it. The home services category is reactive — when one painter runs a "Save 10%" offer, the rest match within a week. Be the one who triggers the response, not the one who reacts.

---

## Weekly sweep (15 min, every Monday)

### Step 1 — Search Meta Ad Library (5 min)

URL: `https://www.facebook.com/ads/library/?active_status=active&ad_type=all&country=CA`

**Searches to run (one tab each):**
1. `painters toronto` — broad Toronto market
2. `house painting mississauga` — west GTA
3. `condo painting toronto` — vertical
4. `cabinet painting toronto` — niche
5. `arcan painting` — your own brand (defensive monitoring)
6. `platinum pro painters` — top competitor
7. `wow 1 day painting` — top franchise competitor
8. `sienna painting toronto` — premium competitor
9. `john bell painting` — legacy competitor
10. `probrush` / `probrush4` — niche competitor

**Newer competitors to monitor (added 2026-06-01 from the 4-agent audit):**
11. `home painters toronto` — 5,872 HomeStars reviews, dominant by review count
12. `paint it up` — newer entrant, watch for fast growth
13. `executive touch painters` — newer entrant
14. `encore painting` — newer entrant
15. `bright painting` — newer entrant
16. `virat painting` — newer entrant

**Original competitors from first scan:**
- `student works don valley` / `scarborough student painters` — student labor model
- `lavish construction` — popcorn ceiling specialist
- `pool painting pros` — pool coating specialist (niche)
- `probrush4` — windows/doors/soffit specialist (niche)

**For each search, record:**
- Total active ads
- New ads in last 7 days (the most useful signal)
- Any with "Limited time" / "Save X%" / "Book now" — these are conversion-optimized
- Any with video (video is the meta-trend, even in boring verticals)
- Any new angle you haven't seen before

### Step 2 — Note new competitor ads in `COMPETITIVE-NOTES.md`

Create and maintain a weekly log:

```
## 2026-06-08 — Week 1

### New this week
- **Platinum Pro:** launched "Save 15% on cabinet painting" (June 6)
- **Student Works Don Valley:** started running video testimonials (June 5)
- **WOW 1 Day:** new ad set with "Same-day painting, no mess" (June 7)

### Notable copy
- "Book this week, paint next week" — Student Works (urgency angle)
- "No deposit required" — Platinum Pro (trust/lower-friction)
- "5-year warranty" — Sienna (longer than Arcan's 2-year)

### Notable imagery
- 3 competitors now using before/after carousels
- 1 using crew photos (Sienna)
- 0 using family/owner photos (Arcan's Family Story campaign is differentiated)

### What we should do
- **Counter Platinum Pro's 15% off** by leading with warranty + family (don't match on price)
- **Match Sienna's 5-year warranty?** No — Arcan's 2-year is sufficient, and matching = commodity
- **Test "No deposit required" copy** in our ads (test variant next week)
- **Maintain the family angle** — no competitor is doing this
```

Search "house painting" + "Toronto" filter. Note:

### Step 5 — Quick review (3 min)

In the same weekly session, ask:
- Is any competitor outspending us significantly? (rough signal: ad count × duration in library)
- Is any competitor running an offer we should match?
- Is any competitor using an angle we should borrow?
- Is any competitor doing something we should counter?

## Monthly deep-dive (45 min, first Monday of month)

### Full competitor teardown
For the top 3-5 competitors, do a fresh teardown:
- All active ads in Meta library
- Landing pages — screenshot, screenshot, screenshot
- Google Ads presence — what keywords, what copy
- Google Business Profile — reviews, photos, hours
- Social presence — Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn

### Update `COMPETITOR-PROFILES.md`

For each top competitor:

```
## Platinum Pro Painters

**Founded:** ~2014
**Owner:** unknown
**Volume:** high (5+ active ad variants as of 2026-06-01)
**Specialty:** Cabinet painting, refacing, refinishing
**Pricing tier:** premium ($3K-8K typical job)
**Unique angle:** Financeit payment plans, 10% off promo

**Their weaknesses:**
- No "family" or "trust" story (corporate feel)
- Heavy on discounts (commodity risk)
- Cabinet-only niche (limited cross-sell)

**Our counter:**
- Lead with family + 2-year warranty (we win on trust)
- Don't match their 10% off (we'd lose margin)
- Target their missed segment: full-home interiors (we have it, they don't push it)
```

### Note seasonal trends
- January-February: budget season, "new year new room" messaging peaks
- March-April: exterior season opens, "spring refresh" angle
- May-June: AGM season for condos, B2B opportunity
- July-August: tenant turnover rush, property manager outreach
- September-October: "fall touch-up" + winter prep
- November-December: year-end budget spend (commercial), "holiday gift" (gift certificates)

**Plan your creative refresh calendar to these seasons** — see AD-CREATIVE.md.

## Trigger events (act within 24 hours)

Some competitor moves require immediate response:

### Trigger 1: A competitor launches an offer we don't match
- They run "Save 20%" for the week
- We don't match the % — we counter with "no discount, but here's our 2-year warranty" in a new ad
- New ad live in 24 hours
- This is more about narrative than margin

### Trigger 2: A competitor wins a major account we were bidding on
- Reach out to that account's decision-maker directly
- Send a "congrats on the choice" + "we'd love to be your backup painter" email
- 30% of these convert within 6 months when the chosen vendor slips

### Trigger 3: A new competitor enters the GTA market
- New Ad Library entries with GTA targeting
- Add to weekly monitoring immediately
- Don't panic — most new entrants burn out in 90 days

### Trigger 4: A competitor's ad goes viral (unusual for this category)
- 100K+ impressions on a single ad
- Analyze the angle and consider borrowing (with our own twist)
- Don't copy verbatim — make it ours

## Tools

- **Meta Ad Library** (free) — primary source
- **Google Trends** (free) — search volume trends
- **Google Search incognito** — see who's bidding on head terms
- **Ahrefs / SEMrush** (paid, $99+/mo) — for deep SEO + paid keyword intel. Skip for v1.
- **Local Falcon / BrightLocal** (paid, $30+/mo) — local SEO tracking. Skip for v1.
- **SimilarWeb** (free tier) — rough traffic estimates. Useful for spotting which competitors are getting organic momentum.

For v1, the free stack is enough. Re-evaluate paid tools at month 3 if the campaigns are profitable.

---

## What you DON'T do

- Don't obsess over competitors. 80% of optimization should be on your own ads, not on copying competitors.
- Don't react to every small move. Most competitor ads run for 2-3 weeks then change.
- Don't try to match every offer. Compete on different axes.
- Don't badmouth competitors in your ads ("Unlike [competitor], we..."). Looks petty. Wins trust with nobody.

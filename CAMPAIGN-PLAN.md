## Why these 5 campaigns

1. **Meta "Single Room Refresh"** — high-intent, low-friction entry offer. Largest warm lead pool.
2. **Meta "Real Estate Pre-Listing"** — niche B2B, high-value (avg $5K-15K), competitor-overlap with Platinum Pro / Sienna.
3. **Meta "WSIB + Warranty" Retargeting** — pulls back site visitors. Cheapest CPL.
4. **Meta "Family Story" (NEW)** — leverages the Cañabate family photo. Builds emotional brand layer that no competitor has.
5. **Meta "Before/After Trust" Carousel (NEW)** — addresses the #1 trust gap in painting: "will it look like the picture?" Production: shoot "before" photos on every job.
6. **Google "WOW 1 Day Steal" Brand Conquest** — captures broken-funnel traffic. WOW 1 Day's `/toronto-painting/` 404s.

We are NOT running:
- Brand awareness / video views (no budget)
- LinkedIn (B2B condo / property manager is in `arcan-painting` cold-email skill)
- TikTok (GTA 35-65 homeowners aren't on it for painting decisions)
- Broad "painters Toronto" head term (WOW 1 Day + John Bell have 30-year brand moats)
